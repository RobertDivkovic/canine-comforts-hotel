Accommodations

At Canine Comforts Hotel, we offer a variety of comfortable and secure accommodations to meet the unique needs of every dog. Whether your dog prefers a cozy indoor suite or an outdoor play area, we have the perfect option for them.

Acommodation Options

Indoor Suites

Our Indoor Suites provide a cozy and secure environment for your dog, featuring spacious 6x6 foot rooms equipped with comfortable bedding and climate control to ensure a pleasant stay. Each suite includes access to a private outdoor run for exercise and fresh air.

Exclusive Suites

The Exclusive Suites offer enhanced comfort and space with 8x8 foot rooms, premium bedding, and individual climate control. These suites include additional amenities such as in-room toys, a daily photo update, and a private outdoor run, ensuring your dog enjoys a luxurious and personalized experience.

Anti Stress Suites

Designed to create a calming environment, our Anti Stress Suites feature 7x7 foot rooms with soundproofing and aromatherapy diffusers. These suites come with orthopedic bedding, soft lighting, and soothing music to help reduce anxiety and stress, making it an ideal choice for dogs needing extra comfort and tranquility.

Royal Suites

The Royal Suites provide the ultimate in luxury with expansive 10x10 foot rooms, high-end bedding, and individual climate control. These suites offer exclusive amenities such as a private webcam for owners to check in, a daily grooming session, and a personal playtime area. Your dog will feel like royalty in this opulent and spacious accommodation.
